# BMW Global Sales Analysis 2018-2025

## Project Overview
Analysis of BMW global sales data from 2018 to 2025 using SQL and Power BI.

## Tools Used
- SQL Server (SSMS) — Data Analysis & KPI Queries
- Power BI — Interactive Dashboard & Visualizations
- Excel — Data Validation

## Key Insights
- Total Revenue: €1,571bn
- Best Selling Model: iX
- Average Price Per Model: €63,977

## Dashboard Includes
- Sales Trend Over Years
- Revenue by Region
- Units Sold by Model
- BEV Share Growth 2018-2025

**##PROJECT INSIGHTS**

1. Electric is Rising
BEV share has grown consistently every year showing customers are shifting towards electric BMWs
2. Premium Models Drive Revenue
X7 is the most expensive model and contributes heavily to overall revenue despite lower unit sales
3. iX Defies the Price Rule
Usually cheaper models sell more but iX is both expensive and best selling — shows strong brand trust in BMW electric
4. Seasonal Patterns
Sales likely spike in certain months — dealerships push hard at year end
5. Regional Dominance
One or two regions likely contribute majority of total revenue
